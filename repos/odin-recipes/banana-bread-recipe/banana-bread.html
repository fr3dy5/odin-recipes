Ingredients
  • Baking Soda
  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Brown Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Bananas

Recipe Steps

  1. Step 1: Combine the dry ingredients in one bowl.
  2. Step 2: Beat the butter and sugar in another bowl. Add the eggs and mashed bananas
  3. Step 3: Add the wet mixture into the dry mix
  4. Step 4: Pour the batter into a loaf pan and bake in a preheated oven.
